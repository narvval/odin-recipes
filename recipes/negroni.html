Negroni

Description

The perfect trio.

Ingredients

  • campari 2oz
  • gin 2oz
  • vermouth (red) 2oz

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients directly into your glass
  2. Mix the ingredients in your glass with lots of ice (a big round chunk, if you've got it!)
    • This is quite a strong drink, so you'll actually want to dilute it a bit
  3. Decorate with an orange wedge
